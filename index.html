About

This is where you will gather together your evidence. Why should we care about this issue? It is a good idea to start with an anecdote and this section will be graded on investment and enthusiasm. Remember, people are moved by opinions and passion. You may want to incorporate a story that will influence people to care. You can add images and videos that speak to this anecdote, either in the portfolio section, where you should have a mix of mediums, including images, videos, evidence, and calls to action, or you can add one powerful image or video here by copying and pasting the code into this divider (I recommend the former so that it is not too busy). Once you have captured your audience's attention and got them invested, introduce your main argument and scholars. This section should include the "so-what?" of your argument. This container will expand as needed but still strive for concision.
